Quick PMQs

Prime Minister's Questions (often abbreviated to PMQs) is a session held in the House of Commons every Wednesday afternoon where the Prime Minister (David Cameron) answers questions from MPs; specifically the Leader of the Opposition (Jeremy Corbyn).

Quick PMQs offers short summaries of these interactions, attempting to focus on the heart of the issues being discussed by removing political rhetoric or personal attacks.
